GirlUpVirago

Girls have a tremendous, often untapped power to change the world. Girl Up helps girls access their inner power to advance the skills, rights, and opportunities of girls everywhere. Our leadership development program transforms girls by exposing them to new possibilities, improving their sense of confidence, and positioning them to be changemakers in their communities. Girl Up has developed the leadership skills of 58,000 girls through 3,300 Clubs in nearly 120 countries, inspiring a generation of girls to be a force for gender equality and social change.
